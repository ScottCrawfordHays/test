South Africa

A world of experiences in one country

← All Destinations

South Africa is one of the most diverse and dramatic destinations on earth. Within a single trip you can experience a Big Five safari in Kruger National Park, explore the extraordinary cultural life of Johannesburg, drive the breathtaking Garden Route, and finish in Cape Town — consistently voted one of the world's most beautiful cities. South Africa's combination of wildlife, landscapes, world-class wine, and powerful history creates a depth of experience that few destinations can match.

✈️Holiday Destination
🗓️Suggested: 14–21 nights

Key Highlights

🦁

Kruger National Park

One of Africa's great game reserves — home to the Big Five and easily combined with a luxury private game lodge stay.

🏔️

Table Mountain

Cape Town's iconic flat-topped landmark offers spectacular views across the city, peninsula and ocean.

🍷

Cape Winelands

Stellenbosch and Franschhoek produce some of the southern hemisphere's finest wines, set against gorgeous mountain scenery.

🌊

The Garden Route

A spectacular 300-kilometre coastal drive from Mossel Bay to Storms River, with forests, beaches and wildlife en route.

Best Time to Visit

May to September is ideal for game viewing in Kruger. For Cape Town and the Winelands, October to April is best. The country's vast size means different regions suit different times of year.

Example Experiences

  • Track the Big Five on morning and evening game drives from a private lodge in the Kruger area
  • Take the cable car to the summit of Table Mountain at sunrise for golden-hour views
  • Spend a day wine tasting in Franschhoek, South Africa's gourmet capital
  • Cage dive with great white sharks off the coast near Gansbaai
🗓️
