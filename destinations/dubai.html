Dubai

Where ambition becomes reality

← All Destinations

Dubai is one of the world's most extraordinary cities — a place that has transformed from a desert trading post into a global metropolis in just a few decades. The city is defined by superlatives: the world's tallest building, its largest shopping mall, and some of the most audacious architectural projects ever conceived. Yet beneath the spectacle, there's genuine culture to discover in the gold and spice souks of Deira and the historic Al Fahidi district.

✈️Holiday Destination
🗓️Suggested: 7–10 nights

Key Highlights

🏙️

The Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building at 828 metres — ascend to the observation deck for views that defy belief.

🏜️

Desert Safari

Dune bashing, camel rides and a Bedouin camp dinner under the stars — the desert experience is an essential part of Dubai.

🛍️

World-Class Shopping

From the traditional Gold Souk to the Dubai Mall with its indoor ski slope, shopping here is genuinely spectacular.

⛵

Dubai Creek

Take a traditional abra water taxi across the historic Creek and explore the atmospheric spice and gold souks of old Deira.

Best Time to Visit

October to April offers the most comfortable temperatures, typically ranging from 20°C to 30°C — ideal for sightseeing, beach days and outdoor dining. May to September can be extremely hot, though all indoor attractions remain fully air-conditioned.

Example Experiences

  • Watch the Dubai Fountain show from the Burj Khalifa observation deck at sunset
  • Take an evening desert safari with dune bashing, camel ride and traditional dinner
  • Explore the historic Al Fahidi neighbourhood and Dubai Museum at your own pace
  • Visit the spice souk and gold souk on the historic Deira side of Dubai Creek
🗓️

Suggested Trip Duration

7–10 nights
