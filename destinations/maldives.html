Maldives

The ultimate Indian Ocean escape

← All Destinations

The Maldives is the very definition of a luxury island escape. Spread across 26 coral atolls in the Indian Ocean, this collection of more than 1,000 islands offers extraordinary natural beauty — crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, pure white sand beaches and some of the world's finest coral reefs. The Maldives pioneered the overwater villa concept, and staying in one remains a bucket-list experience for millions of travellers.

✈️Holiday Destination
🗓️Suggested: 7–10 nights

Key Highlights

🏠

Overwater Villas

Step directly from your private villa into a warm Indian Ocean lagoon — the quintessential Maldives experience.

🐠

Marine Life

Snorkel or dive with manta rays, whale sharks, sea turtles and vibrant reef fish in extraordinary water clarity.

🏖️

Private Beaches

Many resorts occupy their own private island, offering complete seclusion and uninterrupted horizon views.

🤿

World-Class Diving

Consistently rated among the world's top diving destinations, with spectacular drop-offs and drift dives.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from November to April offers the best conditions — calm seas, low humidity and abundant sunshine. May to October brings the south-west monsoon with more rain, though resorts remain open and prices are considerably lower.

Example Experiences

  • Watch the sunset from a private sandbank with a glass of champagne
  • Snorkel the house reef at dawn to spot nurse sharks and sea turtles
  • Enjoy a private dining experience on the beach under a canopy of stars
  • Take a sunrise dolphin-watching cruise across the open Indian Ocean
🗓️
