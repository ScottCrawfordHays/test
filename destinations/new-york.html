New York

The city that never stops surprising you

← All Destinations

New York City is one of the world's great travel experiences — a place of relentless energy, extraordinary cultural richness and genuine neighbourhoods that each feel like a city within a city. From the iconic sights of Manhattan to the creative energy of Brooklyn, New York rewards walkers and explorers. World-class museums, a dining scene of extraordinary breadth, Broadway shows, and the simple pleasure of walking across the Brooklyn Bridge at golden hour all compete for your time.

✈️Holiday Destination
🗓️Suggested: 7–10 nights

Key Highlights

🗽

Iconic Landmarks

The Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, Brooklyn Bridge and Central Park are as spectacular in person as you imagine.

🎭

Broadway

New York's theatre scene is unmatched anywhere in the world — booking a Broadway show is an absolute essential.

🖼️

World-Class Museums

The Metropolitan Museum of Art, MoMA and the American Museum of Natural History are among the world's very finest.

🍕

Food Culture

From classic New York pizza slices to Michelin-starred restaurants, eating your way through New York is an adventure.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) offer the most pleasant temperatures. Summer can be hot and humid but the city is at its most vibrant. December brings a magical festive atmosphere, though also peak crowds and higher prices.

Example Experiences

  • Walk the High Line from Chelsea to the Meatpacking District on a sunny afternoon
  • Take the free Staten Island Ferry for close-up views of the Statue of Liberty
  • Spend a morning in Central Park and an evening at a Broadway show — the definitive New York day
  • Explore Brooklyn's celebrated food scene in DUMBO and Williamsburg
🗓️
